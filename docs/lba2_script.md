# LBA2 STORYLINE
**Corrections from February 2, 1996**

---

## Chapter 1
* **The opening incident**: Twinsen is at home with Zoe, who is pregnant. Outside, the Dinofly has been injured. It is raining...
* **The quest for a remedy**: Twinsen decides to go to the pharmacy to find something to treat it. The product is not available, so a customer advises him to go to the island of the **White Leaf Desert**.
* **Weather blockade**: At the port, Twinsen learns that traffic has been interrupted because of a storm. He is told to find the **weather mage**.
* **The Lighthouse**: After finding the mage, they head to the lighthouse to cast a spell, but the building is closed and the keeper has disappeared to the north of the island.
* **The Tralü Cave**: To free the keeper, Twinsen must recover his equipment:
    * **The ball**: In the cellar of his house.
    * **The tunic and the medallion**: Lent by Mlle Bloop's private museum.
* **Liberation**: Equipped, Twinsen frees the keeper (while facing the Tralü, a "big boss").
* **The Esmer arrival**: The spell is cast, the storm calms down. The **Esmers** land, explaining that they were waiting for the storm to end before landing. They mingle with the locals.

### Chapter 1 Side Items
* **Holomap**: Twinsen's cellar.
* **Mecha-penguins**: In the bazaars.
* **Ferry Ticket**: Citadel - Desert travel agency.
* **Mushrooms**: Cave (help against the Tralü).
* **Umbrella**: Stolen from the pharmacy / traded with the healer's maid.
* **Fake tunic**: Stolen from the museum (used later as a diversion).

---

## Chapter 2
* **New gadgets**: Twinsen receives a message from **Baldino** about a new **Protopack** (2) and a **portable radio** (1).
* **Zoe's car**: Zoe is tinkering with a racing car but is waiting for a part from Baldino.
* **Disappearances**: Twinsen arrives in the Desert but learns that the healer mage has disappeared, like other mages.
* **Esmer surveillance**: The Esmers are everywhere. Although harmless on the surface, an "Esmer S.B.I" follows Twinsen as soon as he is alone. The Fishbulls are aggressive.
* **The School of Magic**: Twinsen must enter it to heal his Dinofly and investigate.
    * **Druid Trial**: Find balsam (a rare plant). Reward: **The Conch** (3) to heal the Dinofly.
    * **Memory Trial**: At the Slate Dome (accessible via Dinofly). Reward: **Magic Slate** (5).
    * **Dexterity Trial**: In the school. Reward: **Blowpipe** (6).
* **Diploma**: Once the trials are completed, the Minister of Magic hands over the diploma and the **shield** spell (7), unlocking the **green ball**.
* **Infiltration**: Disguised (hat and false beard), Twinsen goes to the Hacienda, a suspicious place tied to the disappearances.

---

## Chapter 3
* **Departure for Zeelich**: From the Hacienda, Twinsen is taken to the Esmers' secret base and then transported to the planet **Zeelich**.
* **Capture**: At the Otringal Astroport, he is recognized by **Joe the Leprechaun** and immediately imprisoned.
* **Escape**: Twinsen must escape with Joe's help (diversion).
* **Encounter**: He meets the weather mage, who is also a prisoner. The mage gives him **Magnagna** to complete the lightning spell.
* **Language barrier**: Twinsen does not understand the Esmer language (no translator).
* **Sabotage**: The Esmers let Twinsen steal a sabotaged shuttle to simulate an accident when he returns to Twinsun.

---

## Chapter 4
* **The crash**: The shuttle crashes on Citadel Island. Joe the Leprechaun survives the crash.
* **Joe's secret**: By saving Joe, Twinsen learns of the **Sendell Ball** (12), hidden in the sewers.
* **Situation on Twinsun**: Zoe announces that children have been abducted. Baldino has left for Emerald Moon.
* **Final equipment**: Twinsen retrieves the **Protopack** from the storage locker and completes the **lightning spell** (13) with the weather mage.
* **The sewers**: Thanks to gallium acid and the architect's plans, Twinsen finds the Sendell Ball. He unlocks the **red ball**.
* **Esmer base**: Twinsen infiltrates the base beneath the Temple of Bu. There he finds a **translator** and the code for the **Green Moon** operation.
* **Departure to the Moon**: He heads to Emerald Moon to rescue Baldino.

---

## Chapter 5
* **Emerald Moon**: Twinsen must wear a **spacesuit** to survive.
* **Infiltration**: With the Green Moon code, he explores the base.
* **Revelation**: He frees Baldino, who reveals the evil plan: the Esmers want to crash the moon into Twinsun using a remote-controlled reactor.
* **Escape**: Thanks to Baldino's **Scaphpack**, they reach their shuttle and head back to Zeelich.

---

## Chapter 6
* **Return to Otringal**: Baldino's shuttle is damaged.
* **Upgrade**: Twinsen must find **gazogem** to turn the Protopack into a **Superjetpack**.
* **Hunted**: Twinsen has a price on his head. He gains access to the Forbidden City and Knarta Island (the refinery).
* **Gas layer**: Via a heavily guarded elevator, Twinsen descends beneath the layer of unbreathable gas.

---

## Chapter 7
* **The Blafards**: Twinsen meets this people, who extract gems for the Esmers. He learns the story of **Dark Monk**.
* **Equipment**: He finds the **glove** (10) used to deflect projectiles.
* **The Mosquibees**: By saving their officiant, Twinsen obtains the **Sarbatron** (6) and discovers a secret passage to CX Islet.
* **Fragments**: Twinsen begins collecting the fragments of the four peoples (Knartas, Blafards, Mosquibees, Sups).

---

## Chapter 8
* **CX Islet**: Twinsen infiltrates the control center of the lunar reactor.
* **Imperial Duel**: He faces the Emperor. Before dying, the Emperor whispers the name **FunFrock**.
* **The sword**: Twinsen recovers the Emperor's **sword** (11). The moon begins to move.

---

## Chapter 9
* **The Forbidden City**: With the sword, Twinsen defeats the Hussars and recovers the Sups fragment.
* **The Mages**: Twinsen finds the imprisoned mages. They are forced to cooperate because Dark Monk is holding the children.
* **Ultimate Magic**: By completing the Temple key, Twinsen obtains the **fireball**.

---

## Chapter 10 - Finale
* **Revelation**: Dark Monk reveals himself to be FunFrock and flees into the temple.
* **The Final Battle**: Twinsen fights FunFrock in the temple. The children are saved at the last second by Baldino.
* **The Miracle of Sendell**: Twinsen is teleported to the Sendell well. He channels the energy to stop the moon.
* **Esmer intervention**: As Sendell's energy weakens, rebellious Esmer ships destroy the moon's reactors, sending it back to its orbit.
* **Epilogue**: Final banquet. Peace between the peoples. Joe the Leprechaun is tied to a tree (for his safety). Zoe rocks her baby.

---

## Appendix: Item Details

1.  **Radio**: Twinsen/Baldino/Zoe communication.
2.  **Protopack / Superjetpack**: Upgraded with Gazogem on Zeelich.
3.  **Conch**: Heals Twinsen, the Dinofly, or other allies by "sucking in/spitting out" hearts.
4.  **Dinofly**: Means of transport between the islands of Twinsun.
5.  **Magic Slate**: Stores the plans that have been consulted.
6.  **Blowpipe / Sarbatron**: Fires magic marbles (improved version by the Mosquibees).
7.  **Shield**: Changes the color of the tunic, consumes magic, protects against impacts.
8.  **Route discs**: CD-ROMs used to program space routes.
9.  **Laser pistol**: Long-range weapon (slow reload).
10. **Glove**: Allows enemy fireballs to be sent back.
11. **Sword**: Weapon required to defeat the Hussars and FunFrock.
12. **Sendell Ball**: Allows you to rewatch cutscenes and warns of danger.
13. **Lightning spell**: Deals area damage (cone) and lights candles.

## Chapter Objectives Summary

* **Ch 1**: Find ball/tunic, free keeper, leave Citadel.
* **Ch 2**: Radio, car, become a mage, leave for Zeelich.
* **Ch 3**: Escape, find weather mage, leave Zeelich.
* **Ch 4**: Lightning spell, Sendell Ball, Esmer base, departure to Moon.
* **Ch 5**: Free Baldino, return to Zeelich.
* **Ch 6**: Gazogem, Laser pistol, Knarta fragment, elevator.
* **Ch 7**: Glove, Blafards fragment, Sarbatron, Mosquibees fragment.
* **Ch 8**: Access CX islet, kill the Emperor.
* **Ch 9**: Sups fragment (Forbidden City).
* **Ch 10**: Dark Monk's temple, FunFrock fight, END.
